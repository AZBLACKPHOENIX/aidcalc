How the Calculator Works

The Business Partners Fixed Investments Calculator helps you estimate the returns on your fixed investments. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Input Investment Details

Enter the amount you plan to invest in the "Enter Investment Amount" field. Adjust the investment period using the range input to set the duration of your investment.

Step 2: Real-time Calculation

The calculator performs real-time calculations as you input your investment details. It calculates the Return on Investment (ROI) and the Total Amount Returned based on a fixed interest rate of 5% per month.

Step 3: Cumulative Balance Graph

Below the input fields, you'll find a line graph illustrating the cumulative balance over the selected investment period. The blue line represents the total amount returned, while the green line shows the initial investment amount.

Note: The green line stays constant because it represents the initial investment, while the blue line increases over time as your returns accumulate.

Additional Information

The calculator assumes a fixed interest rate of 5% per month for the investment period. The graph will be a fixed straight line graph
